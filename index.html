Features

Independent

HoloTech does not rely on any additional bulky hardware like headsets and glasses. Just plug and play! This also means that you can just buy one device for alot of people.

Scalable

HoloTech is an one device projector able to effortlessly scale and adapt to multiple situations. Holograms can even be generated behind objects.

Efficient

Holotech has lightning-fast response time, hyperfast rendering power synced with renderfire, and a low resource drain. All while out performing the competition.


Environment

Holo OS is a completely new operating system built for Holotech Projectors. Holo OS is one of the world's first true 3d operating system. Whether you are a developer or a consumer, Holo OS provides a truly immersive desktop experience.

Engine

RenderFire is capable of designing and executing even the most complex of Holographic GUIs in real-time. RenderFire seamlessly communicates with Holo OS to further eliminate latency.

Why Choose Us?

The idea behind HoloTech started out simple... create a projector that is capable of rendering 3D interactive screens without a base. What sets us apart from competitors is that first and foremost, bulky headgear is unheard of at Oblivion Tech. In addition, we don't believe in subscriptions, and extra payments. This means, that if you purchase a HoloTech device, we will provide you with as many free software updates as we can, so you won't need to buy a new projector.

How It Works:

Scores of microscopic touch sensitive light beams are shot into the air by our HoloCamera 360 Lens. When you run an app built for the HoloTech platform, the lens replaces the beams into the ideal location to form the Holographic User Interface (HGUI). The refracted light beams are then manipulated to form the touchable elements (buttons, text fields, forms, etc.).
